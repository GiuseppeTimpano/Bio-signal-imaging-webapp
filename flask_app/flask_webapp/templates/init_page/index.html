Purpouse

Our aim is to create a database with relative interface that can guarantee both access and manipulation of medical data. It is a creation for educational purposes, but we hope that the use of this platform will be improved and can be of support to healthcare worker. Furthermore, the possible integration of AI is a development that should not be underestimated because it is a new frontier in medicine.

About us

We are Giuseppe Timpano and Annamaria Defilippo, two students of the master's degree in Biomedical Engineering at University of Catanzaro. We have carried out this project in teams in order to integrate functions related to both biomedical signals and images.
